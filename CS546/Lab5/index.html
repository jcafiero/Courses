About Jennifer Cafiero

Biography

I was born on September 29th, 1996 to Anne and Stephen Cafiero in JFK hospital. I have an older brother, John, and two younger brothers, Stephen and Christopher. I was raised in the suburbs of Clark, NJ and my first school was a five minute walk from my house.

Most of my life has been spent in school. I have been involved in a lot of extracurriculars over my 21 years of life: Cheerleading, Soccer, a ton of Dance classes, Girl Scouts, the school newspaper, Yearbook, my sorority Theta Phi Alpha, and so many other things I can't think of right now.
